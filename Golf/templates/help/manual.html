Access aidoo

    First time users

    User need to registered with their details first.

  • Access the page on: aidoo.heroku.com
  • Click on Sign Up button
  • Input form fields:

      First name

    1. Input your real forename into the field. If the input is not accepted, read error message for clarification.

      2. Last Name

    2. Input your real surname into the field. If the input is not accepted, read error message for clarification.

      3. Biography

    3. Enter a description of your interests and motivation. This information will be displayed on your profile.

      4. Email

    4. Enter valid email address that you can access for confirmation.

      5. Username

    5. Create a username on the website

      6. Password

    6. Create a unique password.

      7. Confirm Password

    7. Repeat password.

      8. Date of Birth

    8. Enter valid date of birth to confirm you are permitted to use the service.

      9. image of an ID

    9. Upload an image of a legal ID document.

  • Click Sign Up
  • ID needs to be verified. Follow the instructions on screen and check your mailbox.
  • Open the link. And wait for identity check.
  • After identity check, you should be able to Log In as returning user*

Search a job

    Assuming you are logged in and on the homepage: https://aidoo.herokuapp.com/jobs/

  • Type keyword into the textfield and click Search

    You can look up jobs by the keyword specified by the title, by the content.

  • Or you can scroll the post listings and check the jobs yourself.
    1. You can click on “read more” and read the whole description yourself.
    2. You can click on the job title and read more about the job.

Posting a comment

    Assuming you have successfully logged in and found a job you are interested in, after clicking on the job title you can leave a comment on the job post.

  • Click in the field to “Ask a question”
  • Click Send

    • You should see your comment appear right away.
