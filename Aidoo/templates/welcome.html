Volunteering, without hurdles

Your people, your community.

Put on your helping hat

Welcome to the revolution of volunteering, coming to life right in Aberdeen. Aidoo is an online volunteering platform designed to empower communities. We connect volunteers with aid positions and local businesses in their community. With Aidoo, we promise a fulfilling experience and a new outlook on the people you meet every day.

Our crash course

1 Create an account

Follow the sign up form to make a new account. Once you're verified you can add a biography and upload a picture for your profile to better connect with your peers.

2 Get out there

There are two ways to participate in Aidoo. You can either apply to a job or you can create a volunteering opportunity for someone else with our welcome gift.

3 Spend your doos

When you complete a volunteering job, you earn doos. You can use doos to get exciting rewards from the store, gift them, or post a new job and keep the cycle going.

We want to make a difference. Wanna join us?

Joining as a non-profit or charity

As a charity, we give you an allowance you can use the Aidoo platform to connect with volunteers. You can use the platform by providing volunteering opportunities or sponsoring users who need extra help.

Partner with us as a business

We work with local businesses to power our store. Aidoo is the perfect place for businesses to get their name out to the community and and get feedback from real, everyday customers when trying out new products.
