Spend doos in the store

    Assuming you have successfully logged in and are on the homepage https://aidoo.herokuapp.com/jobs/.

    You should also have been active on the website earning doos by accepting and completing jobs. You can exchange the capital for rewards in the store.

  • Click on Store in the navbar. Or click on the in on the homepage.
  • Pick a reward that interests you.
  • You can also check your doos balance on the right side of the navbar.
  • Choose a quantity you want to purchase. Default = 1.

    • There are restrictions to which items you can purchase besides your doos balance: item stock, and per/person limit. You can only buy the limited amount (specified by the benefactor) once. The limit does not refresh after every purchase.

  • Availability of an item is checked with your doos balance, you can only buy items available for you.
  • Click
  • After buying an item your doos get deducted, which you can check by refreshing the site.
  • You should check your mailbox (connected to your account) and look for an email “Aidoo Shop Purchase”.
  • You get a QR code which you will need to redeem your reward.

    • When you get the QR code, you can go to the benefactor’s site to collect your reward.

  • Let benefactor to scan the QR code you got.
  • The benefactor will click and that proves you’re eligible for the reward.
  • The benefactor should hand you your reward.

In case there is an issue, you should contact is via email.

If you want to earn doos you can become a Guardian (superadmin) on the website. If you are interested you should contact us via email.

The guardian's role is to help to resolve reports on the website. After resolution, the guardian is awarded 2 as a reward.

To access the Guardian program you need to have access Profile/GuardiansHub.

  • Help resolve reports. On the guardian site you should see:
    1. If there are not problems on the website, you should see a site with message saying there are no reports.
    2. If there are Tickets assigned to you, you should see clickable buttons and .
      1. Read Report reason description to make yourself familiar with the issue to make decision.
      2. Depending on your decision choose and choose Guilty or Not Guilty by clicking the button.
      3. Now wait for 2 more users to make their judgments.
      4. Your judgement is indicated in the User Decision section.
    3. If there are tickets assigned to you, but the Ticket title says Verdict: Awaiting decision the buttons are disabled as your part is done, however the report itself is not resolved yet.
  • You should patiently wait for the 3rd person to submit their verdict. After that the ticket will dissapear and you should be automatically added 2 to your balance.
